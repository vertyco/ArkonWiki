# Why premium?
Arkon operates on a simple two tier system, free and premium. The free tier is intended to be a way for users to test out most of the main features of the bot or simply use it for free on a single server, while the premium tier is intended to be a way for users to support the project and gain access to more server capacity and advanced features. The premium tier is $10/month and can be purchased through the [Arkon dashboard](https://upgrade.chat/arkon).
